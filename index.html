Data Deletion Request

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

Automatic Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Open Settings
  4. Select "Delete Account"

Manual Request

If you cannot access the app, email us at:

nicolas.mangin.dev@gmail.com

Include your email address used for the account.

Data Removed

We will delete:

  • Your profile information
  • App usage data
  • Any stored preferences

Deletion is processed within 30 days.
